SHIPPING & PROCESSING
Treasure Valley CBD currently ships to the United States only. All of our Shipping options are flat rate including handling. Standard flat rate shipping is free on retail orders over $50 (excludes wholesale orders). If you are looking for a quicker shipment, we do offer an express processing option to get your package sent quicker. If you are within 3 states of Oregon, we recommend you use "Standard Shipping" as most orders will make it in 2-3 days. We are happy to ship to AK and HI, but please contact us about which shipping method to use if you would like an expedited option.

PAYMENT PROCESSING
Treasure Valley CBD accepts payments via Authorize.net - a trusted online payment processor since 1996 that accepts all major Credit, Debit and E-Check transactions domestically. We do not charge any additional transaction fees or have a minimum purchase.

PACKAGING & SALES
We use heat sealed packaging to keep our orders fresh and discreet. All sales are final. We do not offer returns or exchanges. That being said, please contact us if you have an issue and we will try to find a reasonable solution to compensate for your issue.
